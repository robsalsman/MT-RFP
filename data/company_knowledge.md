## About Mission Telecom (Summary)

Mission Telecom is a **nonprofit telecommunications organization** ("nonprofit internet provider") that provides affordable wireless broadband and phone service — up to 70% off market rates — to schools, libraries, nonprofits, and government/social welfare agencies serving under-resourced communities. Surplus revenue is reinvested into social change via its grantmaking arm, **Mission Telecom Giving** ("Risk Capital for Social Change").

- **Network:** Service runs exclusively on the **T-Mobile 5G/4G network** within the United States (coverage in most states plus Puerto Rico and U.S. Virgin Islands; not available in Alaska, Guam, Marshall Islands).
- **Headquarters:** 8310 S Valley Hwy, Ste 300, Englewood, CO 80112.
- **Contact:** info@missiontelecom.org (general), sales@missiontelecom.org (sales), support@missiontelecom.org (support), grants@missiontelecom.org (giving), media@missiontelecom.org (media). Phone: 877-641-9444 (sales/general, per Get Started page); (833) 979-4446 (listed on Broadband Facts labels). Hours: 9am–7pm ET, Monday–Friday. Zoom call booking: scheduler.zoom.us/f/68g7cor5.
- **Leadership:** Mark Colwell, Executive Director. Board Chair: John Schwartz.
- **Pricing highlights:** Phone plans — Amplify Essential $15/line/mo (10 GB), Amplify Unlimited $30/line/mo. Broadband — Power Essential 100 GB Hotspot $20/mo, Power Unlimited Hotspot/Tablet $25/mo, Power Essential 300 GB Fixed Wireless $20/mo, Power Unlimited Fixed Wireless $25/mo. Education plans advertised starting at $9.99/mo per line. No activation fees, no hidden fees, free shipping; 5-year per-line price guarantee on phone plans.
- **Key programs:** Project: Volume Up (library hotspot lending), RESPOND Kit (disaster connectivity), Referral Program, E-Rate gap support, CIPA-compliant filtering at no extra cost.
- **Scale:** Has reached ~20,000 families with affordable broadband (per July 2026 podcast).
- **Memberships:** NDIA, CoSN, SHLB, ARSL, ABOS, NTEN, Nonprofit New York.

## Broadband Plans

Source: https://missiontelecom.org/broadband-plans/

- **Power Essential:** Up to 100 GB of monthly data per device. For job applications, homework, online research, email, community services.
- **Power Unlimited:** No monthly data cap. For community programs, learning, workforce training, daily digital access.
- All plans up to **70% off market rates**; **no activation fees**; **bulk discounts** increase with volume; "one month free" promotional offer (terms apply).

- Eligible/target organizations: mission-driven organizations, educational institutions, nonprofits, libraries, government agencies, social welfare agencies.
- Exact dollar pricing for broadband plans is on the Broadband Facts page (see below): Power Essential 100 GB Hotspot $20/mo; Power Unlimited Hotspot/Tablet $25/mo; Power Essential 300 GB Fixed Wireless $20/mo; Power Unlimited Fixed Wireless $25/mo.

## Phone Plans

Source: https://missiontelecom.org/phone-plans/

- **Amplify Essential — $15 per line, per month.** 10 GB high-speed data; unlimited talk and text; 256 kbps data after cap.
- **Amplify Unlimited — $30 per line, per month.** Unlimited talk, text, and data; 300 GB of hotspot; T-Satellite connectivity.
- **Network:** T-Mobile nationwide 5G/4G — "America's largest, fastest, and most advanced 5G network." Coverage in most states, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands; NOT available in Alaska, Guam, Marshall Islands.
- **Fees/terms:** No activation fees, free shipping, no hidden fees, no data overages. A contract is required; minimum commitment is one month. **Per-line price guaranteed for five years.**
- **International:** 5 GB of mobile data plus talk and text in Canada and Mexico included.
- **Eligibility:** select schools, libraries, nonprofits, and social welfare agencies.
- **Devices:** BYOD — 95% of modern smartphones work; new phones and refurbished "like-new" models available; number porting supported.

## Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)

Source: https://missiontelecom.org/bring-your-own-device/

- Process: "Simply insert our SIM card into your existing device, follow the quick activation steps, and you're online." Most devices connect within minutes.
- Works with hotspots, routers, and tablets (and per the Phone Plans page, ~95% of modern smartphones).
- Benefits: fastest setup, cost savings using devices you already own, reduces e-waste, teams keep familiar equipment; designed for libraries, nonprofits, and schools.
- Detailed compatibility/unlock/IMEI-check specifics are not published on the page — contact info@missiontelecom.org or use Get Started.

Source: https://missiontelecom.org/who-we-serve/education/

- Plans up to 70% off market rates, **starting at $9.99/month per line**; unlimited wireless broadband on America's largest, fastest 5G network; no long-term contracts.
